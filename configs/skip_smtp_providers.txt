# providers that accept the SMTP handshake and then bounce afterwards
# (Yahoo / AOL / Verizon-class) or any other provider that turns out
# to be unreliable for the customer's mailing-list workload.

# Comments and order are free-form; the loader normalises everything
# to lowercase and de-duplicates.
